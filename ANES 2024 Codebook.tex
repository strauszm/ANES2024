\section{\centering{A note about the data source}}
\textcolor{white}{.}
\vskip.01in

The data in this dataset is drawn from the 2024 wave of the \href{https://electionstudies.org/data-center/2024-time-series-study/}{American National Election Study}, which was conducted both before and after the 2024 presidential election in the United States. \href{https://www.michaelstrausz.com/}{Michael Strausz} selected the variables and their order, and edited the dataset for readability and usability in R. This dataset was downloaded in May 2025; it is a prerelease of the data.
